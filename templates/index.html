Model Transparency & Rules

Details behind our prediction metrics, calibration scales, and boundaries.

XGBoost Classifier

Scores applicant defaults using gradient boosting trees. Evaluated strictly inside cross-validation splits to prevent data leakage.

Isotonic Calibration

Maps raw boosted scores into reliable empirical probabilities. Platt and Isotonic systems were compared, with Isotonic chosen due to its low Brier score (0.0849).

Risk Boundaries

The 0.12 and 0.35 thresholds are policy-selected boundaries supported by out-of-fold training data:
• Low Risk: P < 0.12 (4.7% default rate)
• Medium Risk: 0.12 ≤ P < 0.35
• High Risk: P ≥ 0.35 (79.2% default rate)
